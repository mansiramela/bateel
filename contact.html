Returns & Refunds

As Bateel products are perishable, returns are not possible. However, you may request a replacement or refund instead.

Refunds and replacement requests will be considered if the products were damaged during delivery, the incorrect product was shipped, or products were defective or out of date.
The request must be emailed to customercare@bateel.com within 48 hours of the order receipt with a detailed description and supporting photographs.

Once the request is received and approved, the replacement item(s) will be shipped within 2 to 5 working days.
Where replacements are not possible, a refund will be issued via the original payment method.

Once the refund request is received and approved, a refund will
be issued via the original payment method. Please note, refunds
can take 10 to 30 working days to be reflected in the account.
