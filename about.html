A Pioneer in Gourmet Excellence

Over the years Bateel has transformed dates from a traditional staple to a gourmet delicacy, establishing itself as a leader in the global luxury food industry.

With a commitment to quality and authenticity, Bateel offers an array of products, including organic dates, single-origin chocolates and artisanal gourmet foods, all reflecting the brand's dedication to excellence and innovation.

Welcome to the world of Bateel, where sustainable farming and luxury retailing embrace in harmony.

Bateel produces exceptional quality organic dates. This is the foundation for developing Bateel’s world-renowned gourmet range, offered in Bateel luxury boutiques and other premium retailing channels.

Elevating the Art of Gift-Giving

Bateel is the ultimate indulgence and gifting destination. Our luxurious boutiques offer a distinctly different experience where guests are welcomed with the warmth of Arabian hospitality and treated to a collection of gourmet products.

Bespoke Gifts

Our luxurious gift collections are fully customisable and feature a range of gourmet delights. Tailored to suit a variety of themes and tastes, the gift packaging includes stunning wooden presentation boxes, ballotins, silver trays, etageres and classic carton packaging.

Fine Food,
Exceptional Flavour

Bateel’s market leadership position as a purveyor of fine food is anchored on its strategy of developing innovative high-quality products. An ethos supported by research, production assets and collaborations with other fine food innovators. These investments enable Bateel to continuously enhance the existing range, adding new products to the portfolio, which include date inspired creations, such as date-balsamic vinegar, flavoured date dhibs and sparkling date juice.

Café Bateel

One of the leading purveyors of gourmet food experiences, Café Bateel is a celebration of culinary inspiration. Taking the brand beyond gourmet products, Café Bateel represents the ultimate Bateel experience, providing a warm and sophisticated ambiance where you can enjoy extraordinary cuisine. Café Bateel is recognised for exquisite quality, heartfelt hospitality and gourmet food located in premium commercial and residential districts.

Taste the Difference

Reinforcing the earth-to-plate connection, our menus combine Middle Eastern essence with Mediterranean diversity. With premium, natural flavours as the foundation of every dish. ur award-winning chefs have an unwavering commitment to produce the highest quality dishes sourcing only the finest ingredients to create Mediterranean inspired menu.

Bateel El’an

Bateel El’an is the latest evolution in Bateel’s renowned portfolio, seamlessly blending the brand’s signature quality with a modern, fast-paced dining concept. Designed for today’s dynamic lifestyle, El’an offers a refined yet convenient experience, perfect for those seeking gourmet flavours on the go. ​

A Culinary Experience on the Go

Bateel El’an redefines convenience with a menu that balances quality and speed. From rich, single-origin coffees to freshly baked bread, handcrafted pastries, nourishing salads, and gourmet sandwiches, every item is thoughtfully prepared to deliver exceptional flavour.
