A Gift of Ajwa – A Rare Saudi Treasure

Grown in the heart of Saudi Arabia, Bateel’s Ajwa dates are a symbol of heritage and authenticity. Known as the ‘Holy Date’, this prized variety has been enjoyed for over 8,000 years, cherished for its deep flavour, soft texture, and natural sweetness reminiscent of fine prunes. Ranging in colour from warm, soft brown to deep black, each date is as visually striking as it is exquisite in taste.

Cultivated on Bateel’s own farms and certified organic, every Ajwa date reflects a commitment to exceptional quality and sustainable farming. From tree to table, each step honours a legacy that has made Ajwa one of the most sought-after date varieties in the world.
